Business-Ready Setup

  • Checking WhatsApp connection is managed in the background so business owners do not need Twilio screens.
  • Checking Gemini can help classify messages and draft better replies.
  • Use the public landing page for signup, then send owners straight to the dashboard.
  • Before launch, replace the placeholder Terms and Privacy pages with reviewed documents.
  • For paid onboarding, connect each approved WhatsApp sender to this webhook: /webhook/whatsapp.

Production Checks

  • Set SESSION_SECRET to a long random value.
  • Use COOKIE_SECURE=True on HTTPS hosting.
  • Use Aiven CA certificate with AIVEN_CA_CERT_PATH for strict SSL.
  • Deploy online before connecting real business WhatsApp traffic.
